TEXT-AREA ANSWER EXTRACTION (UPDATED)

For text-area fields:
- Do NOT restrict answer spans by left alignment
- The answer is ALL text spans between:
    - labelA.topEnd
    - labelB.topStart

Extraction rule:
span.page === labelA.page
AND span.top > labelA.topEnd
AND span.top < labelB.topStart

Sort spans by:
- top ascending
- left ascending

Concatenate span.text with spaces to form the final answer.

This must support:
- multi-line answers
- wrapped text
- varying left indentation
